Group Participation Guidelines

Techlahoma collects donations to support user groups by purchasing equipment, food, and supplies.

In order to qualify for support, groups need to meet about a particular topic/tech or have some otherwise concrete reason for meeting and they need at least 3 organizers or volunteers to make sure the group will thrive. Group leaders must accept and enforce the Techlahoma Code of Conduct (or another comprable CoC) and agree to be good community stewards.
